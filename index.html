    Smart Spice

    Always have the spice you need when you need it

Never

Run Out!

Do you remember that time when you were cooking a dinner you craved all day, and you realized that you didn’t have the most important spice? Or that you didn’t have enough of that spice? Or that the spice was 3 years old? The Smart Spice ensures that will never happen again because it tracks your spices for you.

Process

How does this work?

Step 1

Transfer Spices

The Smart Spice comes with a spice rack and 20 specialized jars. You transfer the spices you have at home into the jars and download the app for setup.

Step 2

Mobile App setup

From the mobile application, you assign spices to each jar and setup your preferences for low levels, re-ordering, and organizing your spices.

Step 3

Cook your heart out

The Smart Spice will track your spices and let you know when spices are low or going bad. You can check your spices anywhere: at work, the grocery store, or sitting on the can. PLUS, the unique geometry of the jars ensure they remain in the right order.
